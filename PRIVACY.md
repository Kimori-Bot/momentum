# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Data We Collect

Momentum stores the following data locally on your device:
- Habit names and colors
- Completion dates (for streak tracking)
- App preferences

When you sign up, we collect:
- Email address (for account creation)
- User ID (unique identifier)

## Cloud Sync

Your data is synced to our cloud service (Supabase) to enable:
- Data backup
- Cross-device sync (Premium feature)

## Subscriptions

Subscription payments are processed by Apple/Google through their respective app stores. We do not collect payment information.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app settings, or by contacting support.

For privacy concerns: privacy@momentum-habits.com
